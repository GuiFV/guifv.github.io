Magnetic sensor (Arduino)

A magnetic field distance sensor

I am currently researching about magnetic fields on my spare time, and I needed a device that could measure distance and magnetic filed in one go. To accomplish this I attached a time-of-flight at the same level as a magnetic field sensor (Hal). Also, I was tired of having Arduino projects that are supposed to be handheld be tethered when I needed to test them. So I also created a li-ion battery module with a step-down attached to it and a parallel circuit to charge each cell individually without removing them. Now, this can be used in any Arduino project I make in the future.
