PLANNUS

A software I bought and modified to manage projects

I was tired of the limitations of Trello. I thought Jira was way to convoluted. I had built a project management software from scratch but each new iteration is a lot of work. People love spreadsheets because they are easy to use and understand but they are very limited. I needed something easy to use, quick to implement and scalable. And I wanted to sell it if people wanted to use it on their own business. So I finally did it: I bought a software, changed it a little bit and branded it Plannus. I'm still working on the platform. Let's see what the future holds for it.
