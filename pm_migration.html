IDETRA

Education and project management platform

This is a personal project of mine. I created this platform to absorb volunteers willing to work on a good cause. From the start it was a very complex platform that encompass explaining what the initiative is all about, an education platform and a project management system. Completely build from scratch, I used bootstrap on the front end and Django on the backend. I coded all the frontend and most of the core backend. Eventually I had help to finish some of the more complex functionalities.
