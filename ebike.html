e-Bike battery

Recycling used notebook cells

This project could not be done without my good physicist friend, Alessandro Gontijo. He pushed me into building this, taught me a lot about electronics and li-ion technology. The li-ion tester is his project. The design, specs and how to make 72 cells fit a tight space was mine. I found a way to get a lot of notebook batteries from various places in Rio de Janeiro. And now we were cracking our heads with laser cutters, 3D printing, some wires and a dream. Well, after a few months, my e-bike was alive again. And very, very fast!
