Self-sustainable house

Research on how to use recyclable materials

This was a proof of concept for IDETRA. We wanted to show that is possible to gather materials from trash and use them to produce water, food, energy, fertilizer, and heat your shower. The electrical component of the project was not necessarily recycled but it is know that solar panels are being found intact in the trash. In any case, it is just one example. There are other methods that anyone could do (and some pretty cool ones depending on your geolocation).
