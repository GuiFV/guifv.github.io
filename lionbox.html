Lionbox

The 3-in-1 bench tool

This project started as a simple way to reroute PC ATX power source to a modular bench power source. The idea of using ATX was because people throw away a lot of those when they lose just a little performance. Later I decided to put a li-ion tester and charger. Now I had a big project that took quite a long time to develop, but also would have a very nice tool to: Safely reuse ATX power source computers, output fixed and variable power for electronic projects, test li-ion cells that I recycle from notebook batteries people throw away and charge li-ion cells to use in various projects. The case is a modification from a project on Thingverse that I 3D printed at home. This was made with the precious help of my good friend and physicist, Alessandro Gontijo.
