Spreadsheet-based Project Management

Organize your project "like a pro"

I had a couple of days to create a solution to manage multiple projects for multiple clients. I had different teams and the clients could not see pieces of the information we were sending back and forth. And even worst: I had to make sure the clients could not change sensitive material, but any new team member could. So I divided to conquer: 3 spreadsheets that feed from each other. Green tabs are editable and blue ones have their data coming from somewhere else. Tools that you won't need you can hide or delete. Just don't forget to change the setup tab information to the key of each spreadsheet when you make a copy of the template and protect them by only allowing specific people to view/edit each one. Oh! and I made it all bilingual, so to change everything to english, just go to cell A1 on the second tab and write "en". Enjoy!
